Getting Rid of Sim Toolkit

February 19, 2023 · 1 min · 74 words

I installed GrapheneOS on my phone and was slightly bothered by a sim toolkit app. Here is how to uninstall it. Go to your phone’s settings and locate the build number. Tap seven times on it. Developer mode should now be enabled. Then enable USB debugging. Connect your phone to a computer where adb is installed and run:

adb shell pm uninstall --user 0 com.android.stk

Disable USB debugging and then also disable developer mode.
